Guidelines for optimizing campaign content

What works: Short messages (under 160 chars), strong CTA, urgency/scarcity, personalization, local language, clear bonus offers, casino name + welcome bonus amount.

Test these: Different send times (6-10pm local), sender ID rotation, emoji vs no emoji, question openers vs statement openers.

Avoid: All caps, spammy words (FREE!!!, CLICK NOW), broken links, wrong language for country, generic copy across all GEOs.
